How SunWire handles site usage data.

This page summarizes the main privacy practices visible in the current SunWire codebase, including analytics, advertising integrations, and browser storage used to improve page performance and reading continuity.

Last updated: March 21, 2026

Information We May Process

SunWire may process standard request information such as browser type, device information, referring pages, and general usage activity needed to operate, secure, and improve the site.

Analytics

The site includes Google Analytics page-view tracking. That service may collect usage information according to Google's own policies and settings.

Advertising

SunWire loads Google AdSense ad units on article and homepage templates. Advertising partners may use cookies or similar technologies to deliver, measure, and improve ads.

Browser Storage

The current site code uses local storage and session storage for lightweight site behavior such as remembering homepage state and caching article data for faster navigation.

Your Choices

  • You can control cookies and site data through your browser settings.
  • You can clear local storage and session storage in your browser at any time.
  • You can use Google's own controls for ad and analytics preferences where available.

Policy Scope

This policy applies to SunWire pages and does not control third-party sites, embeds, or services linked from the platform.
